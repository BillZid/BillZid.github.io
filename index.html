About

Bio

I work on large language model pretraining, scaling laws and efficient training/inference recipes. I received my M.S. in NLP from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), and my B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Shandong University.

I enjoy bridging theory (scaling laws, optimization) and practice (data, systems, training at scale). Feel free to reach out if you'd like to collaborate.
